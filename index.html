JAMSHEDPUR PARK


Jubliee Park

Jubilee Park, located in the heart of Jamshedpur, is a sprawling green space spread over 225 acres. It features well-manicured lawns, a lake with boating facilities, a rose garden, and a musical fountain. The park also has an amusement park, a children's play area, and a zoo. It is a popular destination for locals and tourists alike.

Sir Dorabji Tata Park


Sir Dorabji Tata Park is another beautiful park in Jamshedpur, named after the founder of Tata Steel. Spread over 55 acres, the park features well-manicured lawns, colorful flower beds, and a beautiful lake. It also has a walking track, a children's play area, and a musical fountain. The park is a popular destination for morning walkers and joggers and provides a serene atmosphere for visitors to relax and unwind.

Bhatia Park


Bhatia Park is a small but scenic park located in the heart of Jamshedpur. It features lush green lawns, a small pond, and colorful flower beds. The park also has a children's play area, a walking track, and a small outdoor gym. Bhatia Park is a great place for a leisurely stroll or a quick workout, and it provides a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of the city.

Ghari Park


Ghari Park is a beautiful park in Jamshedpur, spread over 30 acres. It features well-manicured lawns, colorful flower beds, and a lake with boating facilities. The park also has a children's play area, a musical fountain, and a jogging track. One of the unique attractions of Ghari Park is the "Jungle Safari," which takes visitors on a ride through a small forested area within the park. It is a popular destination for families and nature lovers.

New Baridih Park


Baridih Park is a popular park located in Jamshedpur. It features lush green lawns, a small lake, and a children's play area. The park also has a musical fountain, a walking track, and a small outdoor gym. Baridih Park is a great place for a morning walk or a picnic with family and friends, and it provides a serene environment for visitors to relax and enjoy the beauty of nature.

Moolgaokar Park


Moolgaokar Park is a small but beautiful park located in the Sakchi area of Jamshedpur. It features well-manicured lawns, colorful flower beds, and a small pond. The park also has a children's play area and a jogging track. Moolgaokar Park is a popular destination for locals, especially in the mornings and evenings, who come here for walks or to relax and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere. The park provides a serene escape from the city's hustle and bustle.
